Vegan Baked Ziti

Description

Prep time: 2.5hrs

Baked Ziti made with penne pasta and vegan mozz. Dairy-free and cholesterol-free

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dried penne pasta
  • 1 24-oz jar marinara sauce
  • fresh basil, approx. 10 leaves
  • 8oz Miyoko's vegan mozz, diced or shredded
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add dried pasta to a large bowl.
  2. Add boiling water to bowl until pasta is covered in about 2in of water.
  3. Let soak for 40 minutes. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  4. Strain pasta.
  5. Toss pasta with jar of marinara, basil leaves, and 3/4 of the vegan mozz.
  6. Pour mixture into baking dish and top with remaining mozz.
  7. Cover dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake for 15 minutes.
  9. Let sit for 15 minutes and add fresh basil before serving.
